Who We Are

Leverage Technologies is a premier provider of Information Technology (IT) consulting, application development, support, and maintenance services. With customers include those at Federal and State levels, as well as commercial entities, we specialize in various technology services and skillsets, as well as human services, and specialized agile services. We are in business to help clients maintain competitive edge by cost-effectively delivering information technology-related products and services including application development, application support, software management, contingent workforce, consulting when and how they need them most.

We endeavor to deliver business value in a world where technology is essential but often misunderstood, where making development a commodity does not deliver desired results. LEVERAGE specializes in projects involving Enterprise Applications Architecture and Data Integration, Enterprise Resource Planning, Service Oriented Architecture, Application Integration, Business to Business Integration, Application Security, Web and Mobile Applications. We offer strong performance-based contract management experience, backed by stringent quality assurance methodologies and in-depth knowledge of IT operations support. Our culture of continuously improving processes makes it easy to provide status, demonstrate progress, identify risks, providing inputs for business process improvement, manage contracts, and execute on time and within budget.

Our Mission

Provide services and solutions which enable our customers to achieve excellence and sustainable competitive edge with our dedication, integrity, and commitment.

Our Vision

To be the most seamless integration partner for the clients we serve and empower our clients with skill, expertise, and support to manage their business processes.

Our Values

Integrity

Acting and taking decisions in a manner that is fair and honest. Following the highest standards of professionalism and being recognised for doing so. Integrity for us means not only financial and intellectual integrity, but encompasses all other forms as are generally understood.

Commitment

On the foundation of Integrity, doing all that is needed to deliver value to all stakeholders. In the process, being accountable for our own actions and decisions, those of our team and those on the part of the organisation for which we are responsible.

Passion

An energetic, intuitive zeal that arises om emotional engagement with the organisation that makes work joyful and inspires each one to give his or her best. A voluntary, spontaneous and relentless pursuit of goals and objectives with the highest level of energy and enthusiasm.

Speed

Responding to internal and external customers with a sense of urgency. Continuously striving to finish before deadlines and choosing the best rhythm to optimize organizational efficiencies.

Seamlessness

Thinking and working together across functional groups, hierarchies, businesses and geographies. Leveraging diverse competencies and perspectives to garner the benefits of synergy while promoting organisational unity through sharing and collaborative efforts.

Diversity

As a certified minority- and women-owned business, Leverage Technologies understands and appreciates the contributions that diverse talent and suppliers make to our company, our economy, and our country. For the reason, Leverage Technologies diligently, pursues diverse talent and suppliers.

The foundation of Leverage Technologies is a culture of Diversity and Inclusion. Our industry expertise is enhanced by the diverse insights of our global workforce, which is the foundation of our diversity and inclusion program strategy.
